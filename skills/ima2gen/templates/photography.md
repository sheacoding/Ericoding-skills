# 摄影风格 提示词模板

## 提示词结构公式

```
[photography genre], [subject], [location/setting],
[camera body and lens], [film/sensor type], [lighting conditions],
[composition technique], [mood/color grade], [technical quality]
```

## 推荐参数

| 参数 | 值 |
|------|----|
| quality | hd |
| size | 1792x1024（横幅）/ 1024x1792（竖幅）/ 1024x1024（方形） |
| style | natural |

## 精选示例提示词

### 胶片街拍
```
Street photography in Hong Kong's Mong Kok district, candid shot of an elderly man
eating noodles at a plastic stool, surrounded by blinking neon signs and hanging
laundry, Leica M6 with 35mm Summicron lens, Kodak Portra 400 film stock, slight
grain, natural available light, decisive moment style, Henri Cartier-Bresson inspired,
authentic and raw
```

### 野生动物
```
Wildlife photography, a Bengal tiger emerging from tall golden savanna grass at dawn,
intense amber eyes making direct contact with camera, shallow depth of field bokeh
background, Nikon D6 with 600mm telephoto lens, natural side lighting, motion
frozen at 1/2000s, National Geographic editorial quality, raw power and beauty
```

### 建筑几何
```
Architectural photography inside the Guggenheim Museum Bilbao, dramatic titanium
curved facade converging to a central skylight, perfect symmetrical composition
looking straight up, long exposure creating silky water effect in fountain,
Hasselblad X2D, tilt-shift 24mm lens, blue hour golden reflections, ultra-sharp
across entire frame
```

### 食品摄影（暗调风）
```
Moody dark food photography, traditional pasta carbonara in a rustic terracotta bowl,
placed on a weathered dark wood surface, single dramatic side light casting deep
shadows, steam rising from the dish, scattered Pecorino cheese and black pepper,
garnish of fresh parsley, rich and appetizing, restaurant editorial style
```

### 长曝光城市
```
Long exposure night photography, busy urban intersection in Tokyo Shibuya, light
trails from countless cars and pedestrians creating ethereal streaks, static
architecture sharp against blurred motion, Sony A7R V on tripod, f/11 aperture,
25 second exposure, blue-black sky, electric signage glow, ultra-detailed cityscape
```

## 关键修饰词库

**相机与镜头：**
- `Leica M11 with 50mm Noctilux` — 顶级德系镜头
- `Hasselblad medium format` — 中画幅质感
- `Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.4` — 人像定焦
- `DJI Mavic drone aerial` — 航拍视角

**胶片/色调：**
- `Kodak Portra 400 film` — 暖调胶片
- `Fujifilm Velvia 50` — 鲜艳色彩
- `Ilford HP5 black and white` — 黑白胶片
- `cross-processed E6 film` — 反转片交叉冲洗

**技术参数（融入提示词增强真实感）：**
- `f/1.8 bokeh` / `f/11 deep focus` / `1/8000s action freeze`
- `ISO 3200 high grain` / `studio 5600K balanced`
