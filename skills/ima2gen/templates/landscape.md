# 风景 / 环境 提示词模板

## 提示词结构公式

```
[time of day] [weather/atmosphere], [landscape type] with [key features],
[foreground element], [background detail], [lighting quality],
[artistic style/camera], [mood], [quality modifiers]
```

## 推荐参数

| 参数 | 值 |
|------|----|
| quality | hd |
| size | 1792x1024（宽屏风景）或 1024x1024（方形） |
| style | vivid（壮观场景）/ natural（纪实摄影） |

## 精选示例提示词

### 日落山脉
```
Golden hour sunset over a jagged mountain range, foreground wildflower meadow
in warm amber light, dramatic cloud formations lit from below in orange and pink,
distant peaks fading into purple haze, long exposure photography style,
16mm wide angle lens, epic landscape photography, National Geographic quality
```

### 北欧峡湾
```
Blue hour mist over Norwegian fjord, still water perfectly mirroring sheer
granite cliffs and pine forests, single traditional wooden red fishing hut on
stilts at the shore, dramatic low clouds threading between peaks, long exposure,
Hasselblad landscape photography, cinematic color grading, ultra-wide angle
```

### 中国水墨山水
```
Traditional Chinese ink painting style, misty mountains emerging from clouds
above winding river valley, pine trees on rocky outcroppings, small pavilion
half-hidden in fog, fishing boat on still water, negative space composition,
Song dynasty landscape painting aesthetic, monochrome with subtle blue-gray wash
```

### 都市夜景
```
Aerial night photography of a dense city skyline, thousands of lights creating
golden river-like patterns through the urban grid, highways as light trails,
skyscraper towers with glowing windows, river reflecting the city lights below,
cinematic tilt-shift effect, high resolution aerial photo, stunning light pollution
```

### 极地极光
```
Breathtaking northern lights aurora borealis in shades of emerald green and
violet dancing above a frozen lake, perfect mirror reflection in ice below,
silhouetted pine forest treeline, star-filled sky with Milky Way visible,
long exposure astrophotography, Iceland winter, otherworldly and majestic
```

## 关键修饰词库

**时间/光线：**
- `blue hour` — 蓝调时刻
- `golden hour` — 黄金时刻
- `midday harsh sun` — 正午强光
- `overcast diffused light` — 阴天散射光
- `stormy dramatic light` — 暴风雨前戏剧光

**氛围：**
- `ethereal mist` / `volumetric fog` / `crystalline clarity`
- `moody and atmospheric` / `serene and meditative`

**相机/镜头：**
- `ultra-wide angle 14mm` / `telephoto compressed perspective`
- `aerial drone photography` / `long exposure motion blur`
